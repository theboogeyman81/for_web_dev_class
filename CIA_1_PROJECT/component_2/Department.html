COMPUTER SCIENCE

The Department of Computer Science at CHRIST (Deemed to be University) offers a strong foundation in computing, blending theory and practical skills. With experienced faculty and modern labs, it promotes innovation, research, and industry exposure. Students engage in projects, internships, and tech events, preparing them for global careers.
